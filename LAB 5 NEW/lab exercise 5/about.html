Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT), formerly known as Kolej Universiti Sains dan Teknologi Malaysia (KUSTEM), is located in Kuala Nerus District, Terengganu, Malaysia. It was officially chartered on 1 February 2007.

UMT began as Universiti Pertanian Malaysia’s Centre for Fisheries and Marine Science at Mengabang Telipot, providing facilities for practical sessions and research. Over the years, it evolved through several name changes before becoming UMT.

Key Milestones & Faculties

Year Event / Faculty
1979 Centre for Fisheries and Marine Science established
1996 Kolej Universiti Sains dan Teknologi Malaysia (KUSTEM) founded
2007 Officially chartered as Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT)
2020 Expanded faculties: Fisheries, Science & Technology, Maritime Studies
